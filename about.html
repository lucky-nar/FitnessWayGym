About Us



WELCOME TO FITNESS WAY GYM

Fitness Way Gym , a state of art gymnasium located at Kodambakkam, Chennai, was started in September 2014. Since then, we are proud to have helped a lot of people in Chennai to discover fitness. We teach them how to enjoy their workouts, while getting healthy and fit with our diversified exercise programs.

Our Gym is specialized in following programs:

1. Cardio Fitness              2. Weight Gain              3. Weight Loss

Our Gym is fully equipped with centralized air conditioning spread across an area of 1200 sq ft. It has been equipped with world-class equipments, to make exercise enjoyable & injury free to its clients. It hosts a range of cardio respiratory fitness equipments & strength training machines to train all parts of your body like Abs, Back, Chest, Legs, Shoulders, Biceps, Triceps. The trainer is certified in fitness & first aid . The music and the ambience add to the flavor and make Fitness Way a great place to workout. Come to the gym and enroll yourself today to Get fit!
